Introduction

Creator’s Garage is an ever-evolving community of learners who always believe in exposing the incredible opportunities available in the world with their skills, understanding and capabilities . Working with the motto TEAM , we identify the need to empower students with the project-based learning experience and therefore , tirelessly accomplish it.

Mission

Our club is dedicated to improvising IOT , Embedded systems, Matlab projects , Robot designing and modelling. To further formulate a learning procedure more efficient than ever , we likewise have 4 sub domains :- SoftX, Robogeeks, Electrohackers and each functioning in their respective areas of specialisation

Vision

Striving to create a community of like-minded individuals where everyone can act together and put up a step Ahead to achieve their intents . Perpetually inclined to impact a project-based learning experience with real-hands-on training.
